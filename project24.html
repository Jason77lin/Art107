Jason's Project 2



This is my project 2 for Art 107. I've taken a turn on my original
idea because I've run into techincal difficulties with the fluid
animations in blender. Here we have a mix between worlds, both
space and earth. In this virtual reality space, contains the earth as
well as other planets in the Milky Way galaxy. As you explore the space,
you begin to notice that the earth is the only planet that is almost
submerged in contaminated waters. This is my take on bringing
awareness to pollution, as a way of saying our planet is slowly dying.
