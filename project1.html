Project 1

Virtual Reality Code

Statement
 The message I’m attempting to convey with this piece is that the earth represents itself and the tree on top represents the lifeforce with the number of leaves determining the time the planet has left. The tree is slowly withering away because there are no longer nutrients for it to sustain life. The barrels are filled with oil and as the earth slowly descends and becomes submerged in it, so the earth begins to absorb the oils to the point where it impairs the tree’s ability to sustain itself.
